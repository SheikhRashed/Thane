'Thane - The Smart City'

  •  One of India's largest & fastest growing districts; a sister city of Mumvai, India's Financial Capital (23 Kms from Mumbai airport & about a hour's drive from all major business districts in Mumbai)
  • Estimated population of 2.6 to 2.8 million (2 million as per census data, 2011; decennial growth rate of 36% per Ficci)
  • Human Development Index of 8.0; 3rd highest in the state (source: Ficci report sept`17)
  • Fastest district GDP growth (8.1% p.a) and projected to contribute to 20% of state GDP by 2050 (Source: The Economic Survey of Maharashtra 2016-17). In comparison, Mumbai is estimated to grow at 4.1% p.a
  • Estimated GDP of $3.78 billion, based on 12-13 GDP of INR 179478 crores (per GOI data), and a 8.1% projected annual growth (per ficci)
